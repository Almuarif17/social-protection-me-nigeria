# Image Note - HGSF Brief 03 Visualizations

## Overview
This folder contains key visualizations from the Home Grown School Feeding Programme (HGSF) Brief 3 analysis. These charts and graphs present important findings about the programme's budget, reach, and operational efficiency.

## Contents

### 01_budget_trend.png
**Description:** Budget trend analysis for the HGSF programme  
**Purpose:** Illustrates the budget allocation and spending patterns over time  
**Use Case:** Visual reference for budget trajectory discussions and presentations

### 02_reach_trend.png
**Description:** Programme reach trend analysis  
**Purpose:** Shows the expansion or contraction of programme coverage and number of beneficiaries over time  
**Use Case:** Demonstrates programme scale and accessibility across regions/periods

### 03_cost_per_meal.png
**Description:** Cost per meal analysis  
**Purpose:** Visualizes the unit cost of providing meals under the programme  
**Use Case:** Essential for cost-effectiveness and efficiency assessments

### 03b_real_value_70_deflated.png
**Description:** Real value (2007-price deflated) cost per meal  
**Purpose:** Shows cost per meal adjusted for inflation to 2007 prices for consistent year-on-year comparison  
**Use Case:** Inflation-adjusted trend analysis to identify real changes in operational costs

### 04_fraud_recovery.png
**Description:** Fraud recovery and audit findings  
**Purpose:** Illustrates incidents and recovery rates related to programme fraud or mismanagement  
**Use Case:** Highlights governance and accountability measures
